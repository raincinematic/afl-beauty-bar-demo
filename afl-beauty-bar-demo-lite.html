The Visionary

Fatema
Love.

From a single dream to one of the most recognized med spa brands in America — Fatema Love built AFL Beauty Bar from the ground up in 2018. A Palestinian refugee who arrived in the United States with nothing but resilience and vision, Fatema turned her story into a sanctuary for all.

Renowned for her meticulous attention to detail and innovative techniques, she has earned the trust of celebrities, socialites, and top influencers across the country. With locations in Miami, New York, Los Angeles, and Atlanta — AFL Beauty Bar has redefined what a luxury med spa experience looks and feels like.

Alongside Tatiana Enis, COO — the operational force behind AFL's national expansion — Fatema has scaled AFL Beauty Bar into a brand that celebrities, socialites, and beauty lovers trust across four major cities.

“AFL is a place where all people come to elevate their life and feel beautiful.”
